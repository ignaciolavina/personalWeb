About Demola

In short, Demola is an international organization that facilitates co-creation of businesses, public sector & universities locally and internationally. Companies bring projects to Demola, and Demola creates transversal teams of university students to work on the projects.

During the working process in Demola, there are many special events, motivational speeches and working collaborations where the different projects are shared and tested by the community in a networking environment.
