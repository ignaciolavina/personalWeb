DEMOLA PROJECT


About Demola:

In short, Demola is an international organization that facilitates the co-creation of businesses, the public sector & universities locally and internationally. Companies bring projects to Demola, and Demola creates transversal teams of university students to work on the projects.

During the working process in Demola, there are many special events, motivational speeches and working collaborations where the different projects are shared and tested by the community in a networking environment.

Digital physiotherapy:

In this project, I teamed up with a group of 4 international students in Finland. Our client Physiotherapia Forte come with the problem that "the patients don't do the recovery exercises at home or they do them with wrong techniques".

The Solution:

Through a process of iteration and discovery, we came up with a solution: an app with the following features:

  • Direct chat with the company
  • Personalized videos of the exercises
  • Reminders for the patients
  • Resources about the recovery exercises and best techniques

Main responsibilities:

  • Prduct Management
  • Manage stakeholders
  • Development (front end)
  • Communication & pitching
  • Achieveing test customers

The result:

As the result of the project, the company was highly satisfied with the project, and bought us the whole solution for applying to their customers
